Who He Is

A Dedicated Advocate & Trusted Property Consultant

Advocate Narender Kumar is a dedicated legal professional and trusted property dealer based in Palwal, Haryana. He provides reliable legal guidance, civil and property-related legal services, documentation support, and professional property consultation with honesty and commitment.

Practicing at the Bar Association, Palwal (Chamber No. 163, Seat No. 216 & 217), he has built his reputation on clear communication, careful documentation, and fair dealing — whether representing a client in court or guiding a family through a property transaction.

  • Civil litigation and property dispute representation
  • Sale deed drafting, registration and legal documentation
  • End-to-end property buying and selling assistance
  • Rental agreements and land verification services
